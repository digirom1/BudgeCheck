How it works

Three steps to financial clarity

No spreadsheets. No guesswork. Describe your finances once and let BudgeCheck handle the rest.

1

Describe your finances

Enter your income, bills, and savings goals. Fixed amounts, percentages of income, or goal-based targets — you set the rules, and an ✉ envelope for each one.

2

Get a pay-period forecast

BudgeCheck generates a forward-looking cashflow forecast aligned to your pay period — weekly, biweekly, monthly, anything. No AI, no guessing. Pure math from your rules.

3

Fill your envelopes in one tap

When payday hits, open BudgeCheck and press allocate. Every ✉ envelope gets the right amount for that period — you confirm, it fills. No math, no manual entry.

What makes it different

Built around your pay period — not a calendar month

Most budgeting apps think in months. BudgeCheck thinks in pay periods — whatever yours looks like.

✉️

Envelope-first design

Every cashflow item maps to an envelope. When you press allocate, the money flows directly into the right envelope — automatically.

📅

Any pay schedule

Weekly, biweekly, semi-monthly, monthly — BudgeCheck forecasts on your actual pay period, not an arbitrary calendar boundary.

📐

Rule-based, not AI-guessed

Your forecast comes from the rules you define. Deterministic, predictable, no machine learning required.

🎯

Goal-based envelopes

Set a savings target — vacation, emergency fund, anything — and BudgeCheck allocates toward it each period until it's hit.

⚡

One-button payday

On payday, review the plan for that period and press allocate. Every envelope fills with the right amount. You stay in control — it does the math.

🔒

No bank linking required

No account credentials, no third-party data brokers. You describe your finances in plain terms. Your numbers stay yours.
